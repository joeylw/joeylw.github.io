about me

hi, i'm joey!

I'm a Film and Media BA Student living in London.

I recently made the decision to go dairy-free after suffering with lactose intolerance for a few years. I have already altered some of my all-time favourite recipes to be dairy-free, and I'm constantly researching new methods and substitutes, so I wanted to share my findings!

Many of my recipes are also consequently vegan and gluten-free which will hopefully-- alongside using cheap, sustainable ingredients-- make sure nobody feels left out!

While I may not have quite cracked the recipe to a dairy free baked camembert yet, I aim to implement clever alternatives to dairy for every occasion so that even the most sensitive of stomachs can enjoy some indulgence.

marmite gnocchi with vegan parmesan

24.06.2020

I grew up idolising Nigella, always in awe of how confidently she paired bold flavours together. This recipe, adapted from her marmite spaghetti, is no exception.

Gnocchi is cheap and filling, making it a perfect base for inventive sauces such as this one! I chose to top this dish with a vegan parmesan alternative, which adds a nutty, creamy element.

Ingredients:

For the gnocchi:

—   250g pack of gnocchi

—   50g vegan butter

—   1 tsp marmite

For the vegan parmesan:

—   110g/¾ cup cashews

—   3 tbsp nutritional yeast

—   ¾ tsp sea salt

—   ¼ tsp garlic powder

Method:

1.   Add all of the vegan parmesan ingredients into a blender or food processor and pulse until it reaches a fine breadcrumb texture. Do not over-mix, or you will start to develop cashew butter.

2.   Cook the gnocchi according to pack instructions.

3.   As soon as the gnocchi has started cooking, start melting the butter in a saucepan before adding the marmite and mixing until totally smooth.

4.   Add a few tablespoons of the gnocchi water into the saucepan and mix with the marmite and butter.

5.   Once cooked, add the gnocchi into the sauce and stir until every piece is completely coated.

6.   Season with black pepper and serve with a generous helping of the vegan parmesan.

7.   Enjoy! The parmesan can be kept in a jar and stored for up to a month, although I doubt it will last that long! For a nice twist, try adding a dash of nutmeg or chilli flakes into the parmesan mixture.
